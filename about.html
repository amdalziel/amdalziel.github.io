About Me

Hello,
I'm Amy Dalziel.

I bring over a decade of experience as a piano performer and teacher, having completed my Master of Music in Piano Performance and Pedagogy. My journey through music has now evolved into an exciting transition into the technology field, where I channel my creativity into front-end development.

I thrive on the challenges of problem-solving, enjoy the dynamics of teamwork, and find immense satisfaction in building projects from the ground up. Recently, I published a book for beginning piano students - Music Theory Inside Out - that provides an engaging, game-based introduction to music theory. My diverse experiences include living in four Canadian provinces, two rewarding years in London, UK, and traveling to a dozen European countries, all of which have enriched my perspective and adaptability.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I stay active playing the pipe organ and raising my energetic 2-year-old son. My passion for exploration and lifelong learning continues to drive me forward in both my personal and professional life.
