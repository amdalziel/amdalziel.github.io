About Me

Hello,
I'm Amy Dalziel.

I'm a software development enthusiast with a unique journey that blends the artistry of music with the logic of coding. Originally from Newfoundland, Canada, I bring over a decade of experience as a piano teacher, holding a Bachelor of Music degree and a Masters in Piano Performance and Pedagogy.

My love for exploration has taken me beyond the realms of music and into the dynamic world of software development. Having lived in four Canadian provinces and spent two years in London, UK, I've embraced diverse cultures and perspectives, enriching both my personal and professional life. Through my travels, I've had the privilege of visiting over 20 countries, fostering a global mindset that I bring to every project.

The transition from the intricate melodies of piano keys to the intricacies of code has been an exhilarating journey. I am passionate about leveraging creativity in problem-solving, and I find joy in crafting elegant solutions to complex challenges.
